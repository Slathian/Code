Musical Industry

and the twisted chains between the lines
Record deals look very lucrative from the outside, yet the grass is always greener as there are plenty of artists locked into deals that despise their contractual situation.
With record labels signing more artists than ever into record deals and music being the most accessible it ever has been, artists are looking for any way to stand out among the crowd.
Prince is one advocate for independence for young artists as he states in an interview with NPR, "I would tell any young artist ... don't sign” also stating “Record contracts are just like — I'm gonna say the word – slavery" (Deggans)
Comparing record contracts to slavery may seem a bit dramatic but there are aspects within these deals that explicitly exploit the artist’s talents for the profit of only the company who they are signed to.
