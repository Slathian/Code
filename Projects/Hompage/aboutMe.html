Who am I?

My name is Josh, I was born in 1998, November 12th. My youngest years took me from America, to Denmark, where I learned the language and started school there until mid Elementary. I came back to America and lived in Lake Oswego Oregon, attending school there until I sought to move to Corvallis Oregon in 2019. In the past 10 years, my mother has found her roots from which she was adopted from where I learned that I am part Marshallese. The Marshallese people live in the South Pacific of the Marshall Islands, and my people are those from Namdrik Atoll where one day I plan to visit.

What do I do?

I am a student in Full Stack Development, a developer and an engineering student. In highschool I was the Lead Mechanical Engineer for my robotics team Lake Monsters #2635 in the Lake Oswego School District, and one day I will pursue my dream to become a Biomedical Engineer specializing in synthetic nerve repair. For now I am focusing on my Full Stack career, doing normal jobs while taking my online classes and making websites like these for experience.
